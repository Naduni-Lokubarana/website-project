Cake


Birthday cake Cake


Ingredients

Flour 250g
sugar 250g
Egg 4
Butter cup 1
Baking powder teaspoon 1

Steps

Get a bowl and put sugar, butter, baking powder and beat well.
Put the mixture into tray and Bake for 20 min or until a skewer inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean


This is a birday cake with a cup Cake.
It is made for small baby birthday party.
It is shape an animal of Rabbit.
Cup cake also made in animal of rabbit shape
Rabbit parts made by a fondant iceing.
cup cake covered by a butter iceing


Buns



Submarine Bun

Steps



1.yeast mixture
 Add:-
Cup of water, 40g sugar, 1 tablespoon of yeast, Mix well and Keep the mixture for 10 minutes.

2.To make the dough
Add:- 500g flour, 1 teasspoon of salt, 2 tablespoon of milk poweder, 1 egg, All things combine well , And add yeast mixture from the step one.

knead about 10-12 minutes and keep warm place until dough in double size


3.Make Buns The dough cut into same size of parts. And then roll in. Brush top with eggs. Bake on celcius 180 about 15 or 20 minutes until golden brown.
